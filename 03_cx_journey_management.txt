WHAT IS IT?
-----------
Journey Management is the practice of designing, monitoring, and continuously improving customer journeys using real data, insights, and operational signals.

Unlike traditional journey MAPPING (one-time visual diagrams), QuestionPro Journey Management treats journeys as LIVING, EVOLVING SYSTEMS that reflect how customers actually move through experiences.

KEY POSITIONING:
Journey Management = Journey Mapping + Real Data + Continuous Improvement + Operational Signals
