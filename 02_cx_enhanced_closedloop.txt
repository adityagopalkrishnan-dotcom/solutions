WHAT IS IT?
-----------
The Enhanced Closed Loop (eCLF) is QuestionPro's ticket-based customer issue resolution system. When a customer gives low feedback scores or negative responses, the eCLF automatically creates a ticket and routes it to the appropriate team for follow-up and resolution.
