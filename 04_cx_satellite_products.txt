CX DIGITAL INTERCEPT
---------------------
What it does: Captures feedback at specific moments in a customer's digital journey — on websites, mobile apps, or other digital touchpoints. Surveys pop up contextually based on user behaviour (e.g., on exit intent, time on page, specific pages visited).

Use cases: Website experience feedback, digital journey understanding, conversion optimization, UX research.

Team: Gargi Ghamandi (PM), Samsul Alam, Asif Ribath, Shakil Ahmed (Dev), Ankita Yadav (QA)

CX REPUTATION
--------------
What it does: Monitors and manages online reputation by aggregating reviews from platforms like Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, etc. Enables teams to respond to reviews, identify trends, and take action on reputation signals.

Use cases: Multi-location businesses, hospitality, retail, healthcare — any org that cares about online review presence.

Team: Gargi Ghamandi (PM), Samsul Alam, Asif Ribath, Shakil Ahmed (Dev), Ankita Yadav (QA)

CX OUTERLOOP (Strategic CLF)
-----------------------------
What it does: The Outerloop is the strategic counterpart to the Closed Loop. Where eCLF resolves individual customer issues (tactical), Outerloop identifies systemic themes across feedback and drives strategic product/process improvements at the organizational level.

Think of it as: eCLF = fix this customer's problem; Outerloop = fix the root cause so it doesn't happen again.

CX CORE METRICS SUPPORTED:
- NPS (Net Promoter Score)
- CSAT (Customer Satisfaction Score)
- CES (Customer Effort Score)
- Custom KPIs
