NON-PROFIT PROGRAM
------------------
QuestionPro offers special discounts to Non-Profit Organizations and NGOs.

Path 1 — Direct (Single User):
Requirements:
- 501(C)(3) certificate (US) / CRA (Canada) / Charity Commission (UK)
- Backlink on homepage with specified HTML text linking to questionpro.com
- Complete a G2 Review (mandatory — no other review sites accepted)

After requirements met: Contact non-profit@questionpro.com for verification and account creation.
Renewal: New G2 review + backlink confirmation required every year.

Path 2 — Via TechSoup:
- QuestionPro donation program provides survey software to eligible US nonprofits, charities, and public libraries
- 2 separate user accounts per organization via TechSoup
- TechSoup sends email with coupon code; user creates free QP account and applies coupon
- TechSoup link: https://www.techsoup.org/products/questionpro-nonprofit-g-49997-
