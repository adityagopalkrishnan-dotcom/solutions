AI Persona Creator / AI Persona Generator under Journey Management in the Customer Experience area.

It lets you automatically create data-driven customer personas from:
- customer feedback
- behavioral patterns
- demographic data

How to use it:
1. Select the workspace
2. Go to Persona in the left navigation
3. Click QuestionPro AI next to Add Persona
4. Enter a brief segment description
5. Choose sections to include, like goals, frustrations, and tools
6. Toggle demographics on or off
7. Click Generate

The generated persona is editable, so you can revise any card after creation.

Customer Experience → Closedloop → Workspace → Ticket → Actions
