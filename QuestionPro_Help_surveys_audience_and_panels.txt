## Communities Recruitment
URL: https://www.questionpro.com/help/community-recruitment.html
Summary: Community recruitment

Community Recruitment With QuestionPro you can use surveys to recruit and create a community of members for continious discovery, and faster insights. How to add a community recruitment question? Go to: Login » Surveys (select a survey) » Edit » Workspace Click on the Add Question button on the last question in the survey. From under Advanced questions, click on the Community Recruitment question under misc question types. The community recruitment question will be added to your survey. Click on Settings and select the community you wish to link the survey with. You can toggle on/off the fields you want to collect data for. Community Recruitment question type should be the last question in the survey. How does community recruitment work? When respondents answer your survey, they will also join the selected community and will be added as members to your community. Respondent will be added to your community as a member. ## Allowing multiple respondents from the same device
URL: https://www.questionpro.com/help/multiple-respondents-from-same-machine.html
Summary: Allowing multiple respondents from the same machine?

Allowing multiple respondents from the same machine? Allowing respondents to take a survey multiple times from the same machine: By default, a survey can be taken multiple times from the same machine. You can have your respondents take the survey as many times as they want. Every time the respondent takes the survey, the respondent needs to close the current browser window and open a new window in order to take the survey again. I have enabled Prevent Ballot Box Stuffing. How can I still allow multiple responses from the same machine? If you have enabled ABBS, respondents can take the survey more than once by deleting the browser cookies. If you have enabled Respondent Tracking along with ABBS, there is a check in place to see if the particular email address has a completed response recorded. If there is a completed response for the particular email address then another response cannot be submitted. If too many respondents are taking the survey from the same PC how to ensure that the cookies do not get cached? If too many respondents are taking the survey from the same PC, before sending the survey, you may append &responseCheck=false tag after the survey link. This will ensure that the cookies do not get cached, and many respondents can take the survey from the same PC. The responsecheck can be appended in 2 ways for Anonymous link: https://www.questionpro.com/t/AKbYYZYoPB?responseCheck=false OR https://www.questionpro.com/a/TakeSurvey?id=5593579&responseCheck=false While sending the tracking link via email we will need to append the &responseCheck=false in the email body. I have appended the &responseCheck=false tag. Will ABBS still work? Yes, ABBS will still work if you have used the Respondent Tracking link for the Survey. The check to avoid multiple responses from the same respondent will be done based on email address. ## Tracking respondents
URL: https://www.questionpro.com/help/tracking-respondents.html
Summary: How to setup survey tracking and track survey respondents to send reminders, follow up surveys and more.

Tracking survey respondents Respondent/Survey Tracking helps tie individual responses back to the respondent. Survey tracking also allows you to better use features such as survey reminders, survey notifications, gift voucher email, etc. much more efficiently. How can I set up Survey Tracking when sending a survey from QuestionPro? Select the surveys you would like to send an invitation forGo To: Distribute » Email » Compose » Click on Insert Survey Link » Tracked Respondent Here is what each setting signifies: 1. Tracked Respondents: This will add a tracked link to the email invitation. The system will track the respondent's details if this link is added. 2. Anonymous: This will add an anonymous link to the email invitation. The system will not track the respondent's details if this link is added. 3. Survey Link Text: This is the hyperlinked text your respondent will see in the survey email invitation. Ensure you have modified this text as per your need before you try to add a survey link to the email invite Can I add a tracked survey link to my website? No, when you add a link to a website, the responses taken using this link will remain anonymous. A suggested solution in such cases (where you are not sending an email invitation to the survey but need to identify the respondent) is to add a question in your survey asking the respondents to enter their email address. How can I set up Survey Tracking when I use my own email program to send the invitations? You can generate a list of unique survey link per respondent using the Export batch option. To know how to set this up go to export batch for external distribution. What are the features I can use when a survey response is tracked? Survey Reminders: You can send reminders targeted to only those respondents who have not yet completed or started the survey Survey Distribution Statistics: You will be able to effectively monitor the status of your survey's distribution Notification Email: You can send a completion email to your respondent. ## Manage Audience Folders
URL: https://www.questionpro.com/help/how-to-manage-audience-folders.html
Summary: Folders - Organize Audience Projects - How to? 

Manage Audience Folders Folders are an efficient way to manage permissions and aid collaboration while working with multiple users. You can share folders within your organization and also use folders to provide secure access or limit access to surveys within your organization. How can I create a folder? To create a folder go to the Audience product. Under 'Audience', My Projects is your default folder. This folder cannot be renamed or deleted. To add a folder, click on the Add Folder button. Enter a name for your folder and create a new folder. You can create as many folders as you like. How can I share a folder? You can share your folders with other users within your organization. Click on Share Folder On the popup, assign permission to the folder and save changes. How can I rename or delete a folder? On the folder you wish to rename or delete, click on the More (3 dots) menu. To rename, select the rename option. Enter the new name and save. To delete the folder, select the delete option. Folder will be deleted permanently (This action cannot be undone!). All surveys within the folder will be moved to the recycle bin. Surveys can be restored to the My Surveys folder. Can I rearrange my folders? Yes. By default, the folders are arranged alphabetically. To rearrange, click on the sort icon on the My Surveys folder and select one of the following options. A - Z Z - A Latest first Oldest first Sort option is available only when there are more than 3 folders. ## Audience Soft Launch
URL: https://www.questionpro.com/help/Audience-Soft-Launch.html
Summary: What is Audience Soft Launch?

What is Audience Soft Launch[BETA]? Audience Soft Launch is a feature that can be made use of by Market Researchers to launch a project to a smaller percentage of the sample to ensure that there are no errors in the survey before launching the project to 100% of the audience. How to soft launch a project? Step 1-Users can select the option to soft launch from the list of statuses available. Step 2-Choose the percentage of the sample to whom the project should be launched. By default this value will be 10% however users will have the option to change it. Step 3-Once you have chosen the percentage of users to launch the product to, click on “Soft Launch” on the pop-up presented. Once 10% of the sample is collected the project will automatically pause and the user will be notified. How to go live after soft launch? Go back to the status dropdown for the selected survey and select 'Live' from the list of available options. ## Respondent anonymity assurance
URL: https://www.questionpro.com/help/respondent-anonymity-assurance-raa.html
Summary: Respondent Anonymity Assurance (RAA)

Respondent Anonymity Assurance (RAA) One of the challenges researchers face is the requirement for two directly conflicting issues The ability to track respondents response status so reminders or follow up emails can be sent, AND For human subjects protocols or for other privacy reasons, ensuring that email identifications not be linked to the response data. From a technical standpoint, if we need to track who has taken the survey and who has not, the survey researcher also implicitly has the ability to track the response for each individual. To overcome this issue, the Respondent Anonymity Assurance has been introduced. QuestionPro asserts that once RAA is enabled on a survey, although computer generated identification numbers for individuals will be generated, the survey researcher will not have access to both the respondent's email address as well as the response data at the same time. for more information please visit: Respondent Anonymity Assurance The Survey should be distributed using QuestionPro Email Management and the Survey Tracking link should be used for distribution. What assertions does QuestionPro make for RAA-enabled surveys? Once the RAA is enabled for a survey, at the request of the survey researcher, it will remain perpetual and cannot be rescinded -- by the researcher or anyone else. The QuestionPro Survey Software will never present a respondent's email address linked to the response data in any of the analysis tools, reports and data downloads. In the exceptionally unlikely event that a breach occurs for any reason, technical or otherwise, QuestionPro will immediately notify the respondent as well as the survey researcher about the issue. If the respondent requests his response data to be removed from the servers as a result of the breach, QuestionPro will comply with the request even if the survey researcher does not agree. Furthermore, QuestionPro will make all reasonable efforts to make sure that such a breach does not occur again. How do I enable RAA for my survey? Go to: Login » Surveys (Select a survey) » Edit » Settings Enable the toggle switch for Respondents Anonymity Assurance On the popup, confirm that you wish to enable RAA. Once enabled, it is not possible to disable RAA. I have tracking information passed as custom variables. Can I hide it? (SSO authenticated survey) Yes. You can hide information stored in custom variables 1-5. To do so, enable the option Mask Custom Variables 1-5 This will hide/block information stored in custom variables 1 to 5. You will still be able to use logic as well as filtering in aggregate. How do I know if a survey I am taking is protected by RAA? For surveys that have RAA enabled, a link to the details about the guarantee will be put on the footer of the survey. This link cannot be removed or added by the survey researcher. It will be automatically added on surveys that have the anonymity guarantee enabled. Once this link is enabled it cannot be removed, changed or edited. How do the Reports look? Any and all data which can be used for respondent identification will be masked using the text: "**RAA Blocked**". Which fields get hidden when RAA is turned on? Following are the fields which get hidden when RAA is turned on: Respondent Email IP Address Country Code Region License This feature is available with the following licenses : Team Edition Research Edition Was the article helpful?
