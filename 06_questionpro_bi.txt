WHAT IS IT?
-----------
QuestionPro BI is QuestionPro's data visualization platform. A BI tool developed natively by QuestionPro, designed to consume data from different QuestionPro products. Easy to use, yet extremely powerful.

Key differentiator: Unlike third-party BI tools, QuestionPro BI is purpose-built to understand survey/CX/EX data structures out of the box — no complex ETL required.

PRODUCTS SUPPORTED:
Research | Advanced | Team Edition | Workforce (EX) surveys | CX
