CORRECT ANSWER / ADDITIONAL CONTEXT:
Test correction: NPS in QuestionPro CX requires setting the benchmark question in CX Survey Settings under the workspace configuration.
PREVIOUS WRONG ANSWER:
In QuestionPro CX, NPS works as a rating question that asks respondents to score their likelihood to recommend on a numeric scale, usually 0–10.

Here’s how it works in the knowledge base:

1. **Question setup**
   - Add it from **Surveys → Edit → Workspace → Add Question**
   - Choose **Advanced question types**
   - Select **Net Promoter Score** under **Customer Satisfaction**

2. **How responses are grouped**
   - **Promoters**
   - **Passives**
   - **Detractors**

   By default on a **0–10*
